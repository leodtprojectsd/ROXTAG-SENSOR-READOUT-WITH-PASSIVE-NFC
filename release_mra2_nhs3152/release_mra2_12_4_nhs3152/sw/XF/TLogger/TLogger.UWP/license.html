Exhibit A

This APP supports the following NHS31xx based demonstrations:

  • Flexfoils with the NHS3100, printed NFC antenna and printed battery.
    • An additonal interface may be added using e.g. SL3S4011FHK;
    • additional sensing may be added using e.g. FXLS897X and a third-party humidity sensor;
  • PCBs contaning the NHS3100 or NHS3152 IC:
    • NHS3100TEMODB: NHS3100 Temperature Monitoring Demo Board
    • NHS3100THADDB: NHS3100 Therapy Adherence Demo Board
    • NHS3152THADDB: NHS3152 Therapy Adherence Demo Board
    • NHS3152DB: NHS3152 Demo Board
    • NHS3100UCODEDB: NHS3100 & UCODE-I2C solution evaluation board
    • NHS3100SENSORDB: NHS3100 & UCODE-I2C & FXLS897X Demo Board
    • NHS3100SENSORBTN: NHS3100 & FXLS897X Demo Board
  • PCBs and/or flexfoils developed by third parties in agreement with NXP and using the NHS31xx demonstration firmware as delivered by NXP.
